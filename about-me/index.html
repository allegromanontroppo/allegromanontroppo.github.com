About Me

I am a fullstack web developer. In the early years of my career I worked with Borland C++ solidly, building Windows applications and services. These days, however, I create websites with Ruby based backend frameworks such as Ruby on Rails, Padrino or Middleman. Previous to this I developed in asp.net but jumped ship to the dynamic, open source world about three years ago as I found it much more productive and fun.

I'm also accomplished in front end work and consider myself highly proficient in JavaScript/CoffeeScript, Angular.js and CSS/SASS. I appreciate good design and type even if I'm hopeless at actual designing.

I am a super keen cyclist and ride with legendarily enlightened and progressive outfit Leningrad Velo

I'm interested in history, world affairs, free markets and opera dilettantism.
